🏫 About May Day High School

May Day High School is a distinguished institution committed to academic excellence and holistic student development. Located in the heart of Jamaica, our school has been nurturing young minds and talents for generations, producing graduates who excel in various fields including the visual arts.

Our Visual Arts Department stands as a beacon of creative expression, providing students with the skills, knowledge, and confidence to pursue their artistic passions. We believe that art education is essential to developing well-rounded individuals who can think critically, express themselves creatively, and contribute meaningfully to society.

👨‍🏫 Meet Our Teachers

Our Visual Arts Department is led by two dedicated educators who bring passion, expertise, and decades of combined experience to our students.

Mr. Robert Patmore

Visual Arts Teacher

Focus: 2D & 3D Art Forms including Mixed Media, Graphic Design, Animation, Sculpture, and Printmaking. Mr. Patmore brings extensive expertise in guiding students through diverse artistic mediums and techniques.

He primarily teaches Upper School and 6th Form students, preparing them for CSEC examinations and helping them develop portfolios for further studies in visual arts and design fields.

📧 robert.patmore@maydayhighschool.com

Ms. Ashley Chambers

Visual Arts Teacher

Focus: 2D Art Forms including Painting, Graphic Design, and Drawing. Ms. Chambers specializes in developing students' skills in traditional and contemporary two-dimensional art techniques.

She teaches both Lower and Upper School students, building foundational skills in younger students while helping senior students refine their techniques and prepare for CSEC Visual Arts examinations.

📧 ashley.chambers@maydayhighschool.com

🎯 Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide quality visual arts education that develops students' creative abilities, technical skills, and critical thinking. We aim to nurture artistic talent while building confidence, discipline, and an appreciation for the visual arts that will serve students throughout their lives.

Our Vision

To be a leading Visual Arts Department in Jamaica, recognized for producing talented artists and creative thinkers who contribute to the cultural and economic development of our nation. We envision a community where every student has the opportunity to discover and develop their artistic potential.

🎨 Our Art Program

The Visual Arts program at May Day High School offers comprehensive training in various artistic disciplines, preparing students for CSEC examinations and future careers in the creative industries.

🖼️

Painting & Drawing

Traditional and contemporary techniques in various media including acrylics, oils, watercolors, and mixed media.

🗿

Sculpture & Ceramics

Three-dimensional art creation using clay, wire, found objects, and other materials.

📷

Photography & Digital Art

Modern digital imaging techniques including photography, graphic design, and digital illustration.

🧵

Textile Design

Exploration of fabric arts including batik, tie-dye, screen printing, and fabric painting.

🖨️

Printmaking

Various printmaking techniques including linocut, woodcut, and screen printing.

📜

Art History & Criticism

Understanding of artistic movements, styles, and critical analysis of visual works.
